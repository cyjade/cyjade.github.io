Animations

Let Them Eat

An animation focused on designing a cohesive space using fully self modeled objects while also hinting at several concepts including gender roles, class, and luxury.

Playground Fun

A plane of existence between childhood and adulthood. Playground Fun is a conceptual piece made for gallery art. It was shown in a group exhibition at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, Bricolage Dynamics, from November 2018 to February 2019.

Sticking the Landing

A determined squirrel eagerly tries to teach themselves how to fly. Made with colored paper, Sticking The Landing focuses on exploring a character's story and struggles while also experimenting with traditional animation practices.
